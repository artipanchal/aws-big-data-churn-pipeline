Cron Job Configuration for Pipeline Automation

The following cron job is configured on the EC2 instance to automate
the execution of the PySpark ETL pipeline.

Schedule:
- Runs every day at 1:00 AM UTC

Cron Entry:
0 1 * * * /home/ec2-user/pipeline/scripts/run_pipeline.sh >> /home/ec2-user/pipeline/logs/pipeline.log 2>&1

Description:
- The cron job triggers the pipeline shell script.
- The script reads raw data from Amazon S3.
- PySpark processes and cleans the data.
- The processed data is written back to Amazon S3.
- Logs are stored for monitoring and debugging.
